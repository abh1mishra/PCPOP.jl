\section{Conclusion}
PCPOP is the latest contribution to the community of polynomial optimization in Julia. It emphasizes on leveraging equality constraints to reduce the complexity of SDP relaxations. It  has specialized algorithms to handle constrints that appear ubiquitously in quantum information. It has a robust and simple to use user-interface that can handle large variety of problem inputs. Atlast, it provides support for symmetry reduction and trace polynomial optimization which is missing in most other polynomial optimization packages.
